Make the perfect Sandwich!

What you will need (1 Portion):

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Some Lettuce
  • 1 Tomato
  • Some Slices of Meat (can be Turkey, Chicken etc. whatever you like)
  • Garlic Sauce

This is how you do it!

  1. Take your Bread slices and spread them with the Garlic Sauce.
  2. Then take you Lettuce and put it on one of the Slices (This will be the bottom one).
  3. Now put the thinly sliced Tomato on the Lettuce.
  4. Lastly put your Meat of Choice on Top and Close the Sandwich with the remaining Slice of Bread.

BONUS TIP: You could now toast your Sandwich and give it some extra crunch and flavor.
